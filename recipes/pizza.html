Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Tomato
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Make dough: Pour warm water into a large bowl; dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, about 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Mix in flour, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and salt until dough pulls away from the sides of the bowl.
  3. Turn onto a lightly floured surface. Knead until smooth, about 8 minutes.
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat; brush the grate with garlic oil.
  5. Top with 1/2 of each of the following: tomato sauce, chopped tomatoes, olives, red peppers, cheese, and basil.
  6. Close the lid and cook until cheese melts. Remove from grill and set aside to cool for a few minutes. Repeat with second piece of dough.
